About Me:

I grew up in Fayetteville, Arkansas. Fun fact, it's #4 on the list of best places to live according to USNews.

I had a career in the military as an electrician and navy diver. After living in various areas around the Pacific Ocean during my service, I landed in Southern California. I appreciate this area for the mountains, beaches, and desserts to explore.

I have a hobby of collecting hobbies. Hobbies I’ve had fall in the range of ♆ diving, 🪂 skydiving, 🏄 surfing, 🏂 snowboarding, 🧘 yoga, 💃🏽🕺 salsa/swing dancing.

I make it a priority to connect with communities involved in sustainability. I’ve been a part of a few projects that were very rewarding because they involve intelligent planning and getting dirty. And I just really enjoy building things with a team!

Now I'm pivoting my career to coding related activities. I believe in continuous self improvement, so I've rebooted with a Data Analytics bootcamp at USC. My main areas of interest are Data Science and Dev Ops.
